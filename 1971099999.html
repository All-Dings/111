Description

At the Age of 24, Alan-Turing published his Famous-Paper On-Comutable-Numbers in 1936.

There he first described his hypothetical Turing-Machine, which is still an important Tool in theoretical Computer-Science Today.

This Movie shows how the Machine works and also explains Turing’s Proof of David-Hilbert’s Decision-Problem.
