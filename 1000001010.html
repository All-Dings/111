Topics: Author, Wolfgang_Schubert

Next I research the Authors of the Book. The first one is Prof. h. c. Wolfgang_Schubert. The studied “Fach-Psychologe” also wears the Title Dr. rer. nat. [1007].

Since 5 November 1999 he is the first Chair-Man und from 2013 to 2016 President of the DGVP [1006].

He also works for the Bonner-Institut-für-Rechts-und-Verkehrs-Psychologie or short BIRVp [1007].
